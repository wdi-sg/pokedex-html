Ground Pokemon

Ground is one of the three basic elemental types along with Fire and Grass, which constitute the three starter Pokémon. This creates a simple triangle to explain the type concept easily to new players. Ground is the most common type with over 100 Pokémon, which are based on a wide variety of fish and other sea-dwelling creatures. As of Generation 6, Ground has been paired with every other type.

There are 143 Water-type Pokémon.
